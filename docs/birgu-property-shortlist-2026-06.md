# Birgu (Vittoriosa) Property Shortlist
**Prepared for the Kent Family | June 2026 | MAL-13**

> **Budget context:** €1,000,000 acquisition envelope (cash purchase). Birgu is an entirely historic, fortified city — every property is a townhouse, house of character, or palazzo. There are no modern apartment blocks and no SDA zones. UCA designation covers most of the peninsula, so stamp-duty relief (0% on first €750k, 1.5% on next €200k) applies broadly — confirm on each individual title. Pre-citizenship: AIP required (no SDA alternative in Birgu). Post-citizenship: wife purchases freely as Maltese citizen.

### Adviser Notes

**AIP / Citizenship:** Birgu has no Special Designated Area (SDA) zones. Pre-citizenship, the couple will need AIP for any purchase — this is the standard track for non-SDA areas. Post-citizenship (wife), the primary residence purchase is unrestricted. Time the purchase to the citizenship timeline where possible.

**UCA:** Most of Birgu is UCA-designated, which triggers the 0%/1.5% stamp-duty bracket. Confirm UCA status on each individual title with the notary before proceeding — do not assume.

**Shell and unconverted properties (B5, B10, B12, B13):** Factor €150,000–€250,000 for a quality conversion on smaller footprints; €300,000–€600,000 for the palazzo (B13). These are the only routes to a truly bespoke Birgu residence at scale within the overall envelope.

**Sensara Malta (check directly):** Two Collachio-area properties — ref V003669 (200 m², two courtyards, adjacent to the Auberge de France and the Norman Window — the most historically significant address available in Birgu) and ref V006839 (4-bed townhouse) — could not be priced by scraping. Contact Sensara Malta directly; both may represent exceptional value given their location.

**Dhalia unverified listing:** A 4-bed HoC in the Collachio at €750,000, with a 150 m² roof terrace and wooden-beam ceilings, circulated on social media via agent Jack Kind, Dhalia, +356 79831152. Could not be confirmed in the current portal inventory — worth a direct call.
