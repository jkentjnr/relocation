## 1. The one financial finding that decides the file

> **"Buy early" only pencils out on an SDA unit. On a character home it does not тАФ so the home you actually want is bought *at the move*, not early, and the ~тВм135тАУ160k of price appreciation you forgo by waiting is the real, quantified cost of insisting on a non-lettable home.**

This falls straight out of Legal's classification (MAL-4):

- **Only two candidates can legally earn interim rent тАФ Candidate 5 (Sliema/St Julian's SDA penthouse) and Candidate 3b (Tas-Sellum SDA).** On those, ~3 years of long-let income offsets the carrying cost *while you also capture appreciation and keep the unit occupied*. Buying early works тАФ at the cost of an apartment/penthouse product (terrace, no garden).
- **The five character homes you actually want (1 Rabat/Mdina, 2 Three Villages, 3a Mellie─зa villa, 4 Birgu, + Gozo) cannot be let in either regime.** Buying them early therefore means **carrying an empty house** (тЙИтВм50k/yr all-in, below) for ~3 years тАФ which is roughly a wash against the appreciation you lock in, *and* it violates the client's stated "never vacant" preference (Q13) and drags in the AIP-secondary route. So for these, the clean answer is **buy at occupation as Vicki's citizen primary residence (Route B)** тАФ no interim income line at all.

**The client's choice is therefore a product choice, priced below:** the garden character-home they want (bought at the move, no income) **vs** a self-carrying SDA apartment bought early (different product). One property cannot be both. Everything else in this memo quantifies that trade.

**Funding:** modelled **both ways per the mandate.** The conclusion is unambiguous тАФ **for a post-exit cash buyer, cash dominates a mortgage** (┬з4). The "probably a mortgage" assumption (Q26) does not survive the A$5тАУ7M sale.

**Two structural cost facts the rent must overcome:**
1. **The lettable options carry the *highest* acquisition tax.** Because an interim-let SDA unit is used as an investment (not an owner-occupied residence) at purchase, it gets **no residence/UCA/FTB relief тАФ full 5% stamp (~тВм50k on тВм1M)**. The character homes, bought as Vicki's primary residence in a UCA, pay only **~тВм1.5тАУ3k duty** (0%/750k + 1.5%/200k, a scheme *extended* in Budget 2026 тАФ see ┬з2). 2. **Gozo is no longer the cheap-to-transact outlier** тАФ the 2% concession lapsed end-2024, so Gozo now pays standard 5% like the mainland (an old farmhouse may recover the gap via UCA relief). It stays off the main list on client preference and a thin interim-let market (MAL-3).

## 4. Funding: cash vs mortgage тАФ cash dominates for a post-exit buyer

Modelled both per the mandate. Representative on a **тВм1.0M** all-in purchase.

| | **Cash (base case)** | **Mortgage тАФ citizen primary residence (Route B)** | **Mortgage тАФ TCN, buy early** |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cash deployed up front | **~тВм1.07M** (full) | 10% deposit + costs тЙИ **тВм166k** | 40% deposit + costs тЙИ **тВм466k** |
| Loan | тВм0 | ~тВм900k @ 90% LTV | ~тВм600k @ 60% LTV |

| Interest tax shield on let income | n/a | **none** under 15% TA24 | **none** under 15% TA24 |

**Verdict тАФ model the base case as CASH.** Reasons compound: (a) the A$5тАУ7M sale (~тВм3.0тАУ4.3M) funds a тВм1M purchase **3тАУ4├Ч over**; (b) new-lending rates are ~2.85тАУ3.1% **and rising** after the 11 Jun 2026 ECB hike; (c) **mortgage interest buys no tax shield** on let income under the 15% TA24 election; (d) post-exit the family may have **little salaried income**, making serviceability тАФ not deposit тАФ the binding mortgage constraint; (e) no Maltese bank account yet, and it is gated on citizenship. **Mortgage has one legitimate use: a bridge if the family must transact *before* the MoveData sale completes.** Otherwise debt adds ~тВм44тАУ52k/yr of cost and rate risk for no offsetting benefit. *(This is the reconciliation the MAL-2 funding gate O3 asks the client to confirm; the numbers point one way.)*

## 6. Home-plus-appreciation тАФ the five non-lettable homes (no interim income)

For C1, C2, C3a, C4 and Gozo the cash-flow model carries **no interim rent**. The decision lever is **when** to buy.

**Route B тАФ buy at occupation (~2029), as Vicki's citizen primary residence.** No permit, buy anywhere, 3.5% residence stamp band, optional 90% citizen-LTV financing (or cash). No carrying cost, no vacant-home risk, respects "never vacant." **You forgo ~3 years of appreciation** тАФ at +5%/yr that is **+15.8% on the price**, i.e. you pay roughly:

| Home | Price 2026 | тЙИ Price 2029 (+15.8%) | **Appreciation forgone by waiting** |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| C1 / C2 (тВм850k) | 850,000 | ~984,000 | **~тВм134,000** |
| C4 (тВм880k) | 880,000 | ~1,019,000 | **~тВм139,000** |
| C3a villa (тВм1.0M) | 1,000,000 | ~1,158,000 | **~тВм158,000** |
| Gozo (тВм750k) | 750,000 | ~868,000 | **~тВм118,000** |

**Route C тАФ buy early (2026), hold empty 3 years.** Locks today's price *and* the specific property, and secures the **UCA stamp relief (~тВм47тАУ48k saved vs full 5% on a тВм900k HoC)**. But the carry roughly cancels the gain:

- Standby maintenance/insurance/utilities/security on an empty house **~тВм12k/yr**
- Opportunity cost of ~тВм1M deployed instead of held in the sale proceeds (~4%) **~тВм40k/yr**
- AIP-secondary permit + vacant-home risk + **violates "never vacant" (Q13)**
- **тЙИ тВм50тАУ52k/yr ├Ч 3 тЙИ тВм150тАУ156k carry** vs **~тВм134тАУ158k appreciation** тЗТ **roughly neutral**, but operationally and legally worse than Route B.

**Conclusion for the homes:** Route B (wait, buy clean at the move) is the recommended path тАФ the appreciation forgone (~тВм134тАУ158k) is the honest, *quantified* price of choosing a non-lettable character home, and it is close to a wash against Route C's carry while avoiding all of Route C's friction. **The only way to *capture* the appreciation without that friction is to make the early-bought unit earn тАФ which requires an SDA (3b/5).** That is the whole trade, now in euros.

**Timing тАФ better news than first feared:** the UCA relief is **not** a hard 31 Dec 2026 sunset тАФ it was **extended in Budget 2026** (confirmed at runtime; Legal MAL-4). So a ~2029 Route-B purchase can **reasonably expect to still capture the ~тВм47тАУ48k UCA saving**, provided the scheme is renewed at each budget (a watch item, not a forced 2026 deadline). This **removes the main financial reason to buy early** on a character home тАФ Route B keeps both the clean citizen-primary-residence route *and* the UCA relief, leaving only the forgone appreciation as its cost. Confirm renewal nearer the move.

## 8. Buy-vs-rent / the structural trade (mandate requirement)

The classic firm comparison is "live in one + let a second income unit" vs "single dual-purpose property" тАФ but **the client has fixed ONE property (MAL-2 ┬з2.1)**, so the live-one/let-one split is off the table. The live trade-off that *remains* is **buy-early-and-let (SDA) vs wait-and-buy-the-home (character)**:

| | **Buy early & let тАФ SDA (C5/C3b)** | **Wait & buy the home тАФ character (C1тАУ4/Gozo)** |
|---|---|---|
| Product | Apartment / penthouse; terrace, **no garden** | **The garden character-home they want** |
| Interim income | **Yes** тАФ ~тВм12тАУ17k/yr net, self-carries | None (family is in Australia until ~2029) |
| Appreciation over 3 yr | **Captured** (~тВм110тАУ158k) | **Forgone** тАФ pay ~тВм134тАУ158k more in 2029 (or buy smaller) |
| Acquisition tax | Full 5% (~тВм50k, no relief) | UCA relief ~тВм1.5тАУ3k duty (0%/750k +1.5%/200k, extended in Budget 2026) |
| Financing | Cash (mortgage cash-flow-negative) | Cash, or 90% citizen-LTV at the move |
| Legal friction | **None** тАФ SDA lets in either regime, today | Clean as citizen primary residence at the move |
| Respects "never vacant" (Q13) | **Yes** (let) | Yes (occupied from day one; not bought early) |
| Citizenship-timing risk | **Hedged** тАФ works even if citizenship slips | Depends on Vicki being a citizen at the move |

**No interim Malta rent line is modelled**, because the whole family moves together only at ~2029 (Q15, Q3) тАФ there is no interim Malta housing need to compare against. The real "rent vs buy" choice is therefore *whether to deploy capital early into a self-carrying SDA unit to capture appreciation+rent, or keep the proceeds invested and buy the home clean at the move.* On these numbers the two are close in pure euros; the **deciding factors are product (garden vs apartment) and citizenship-timing risk**, which is exactly the framing decision Legal and the Editor should put to the client.
