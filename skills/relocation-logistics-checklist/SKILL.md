# Relocation Logistics Checklist

Use this skill to turn a chosen strategy into an executable move plan.

## When to use
- Once a direction is firm enough to plan the move around.

## Method
Build a dependency-ordered checklist across these tracks:
1. **Residency & ID** — register residence, obtain residence documentation / ID, and align with the tax-residency plan.
2. **Banking** — open a Maltese bank account early; it commonly gates the mortgage, which gates the purchase.
3. **Purchase interlock** — sequence promise-of-sale, AIP application (if needed), financing, and final deed against the conveyancing roadmap.
4. **Utilities & connectivity** — electricity/water (ARMS), internet, mobile.
5. **Healthcare** — registration and interim cover for the gap between systems.
6. **The physical move** — shipping/storage of belongings, importing a vehicle, bringing pets (EU pet rules), and timing arrival.
7. **First-month settling** — local registrations, tax number, and any short-let licence application if letting from day one.

## Output
A sequenced checklist split into **before the deed** vs **after arrival**, with dependencies marked and a timeline that interlocks with the conveyancing milestones.

## Caveats
Procedures and document requirements change; confirm current steps with the relevant Maltese authorities and a relocation agent. Order matters more than completeness — surface the gating dependencies clearly.
