# Malta Real-Estate Agencies & Property Portals

Shared reference for the Market Research team (Market Analyst, Locality Specialist, Rental Yield Analyst) to source live listings, asking prices, and rental comparables. **Non-client-specific.** Always cross-check at least two sources and treat asking prices as distinct from achieved prices. Re-verify URLs and standing at runtime.

## Short-let / rental yield context (for the Rental Yield Analyst)

- **MyRent** — https://www.myrent.mt/ — long-let listings and renting guides.
- **Letify** — https://www.letify.mt/ — landlord/rental-tax (TA24) guidance.
- **Malta Tourism Authority (MTA)** — https://mta.com.mt/ — Holiday Furnished Premises (short-let) licensing.
- Airbnb / Booking.com — short-let nightly rates and occupancy signals (model conservatively; check MTA licensability first).

## Official / data sources (authoritative, for the Market Analyst)

- **Malta Tax & Customs Administration (MTCA)** — https://mtca.gov.mt/ — AIP rules, property taxes, rental income tax.
- **Central Bank of Malta** — https://www.centralbankmalta.org/ — interest rates and financial-market data.
- **National Statistics Office (NSO) Malta** — https://nso.gov.mt/ — property transaction and price statistics.
- **Global Property Guide — Malta** — https://www.globalpropertyguide.com/europe/malta — price history and yield benchmarks.

## SDA developments to know (Special Designated Areas)

Tigné Point & Fort Cambridge (Sliema), Portomaso & Pendergardens (St Julian's), Smart City (Kalkara), Mercury Towers & Metropolis (Gzira/Sliema), Fort Chambray & Kempinski Residences (Gozo), Madliena Village, Ta' Monita (Marsascala), Vista Point / Mistra Heights (north). Verify current SDA designation and pricing with the agencies above, as the list and stock change.
